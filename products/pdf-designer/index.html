The 14-day trial gives you full access to all features. No credit card required. At the end of the trial, choose a plan to continue using PDF Designer.

The license is per kintone app. If you need to use PDF Designer in multiple apps, you'll need a license for each app. Contact us for volume discounts.

Can I use it with subtables?

Yes! PDF Designer fully supports kintone subtables. You can expand subtable data across multiple pages with repeating headers and footers.

What PDF formats are supported?

PDF Designer generates standard PDF files compatible with all PDF readers. You can choose A4, Letter, or Legal page sizes in portrait or landscape orientation.
